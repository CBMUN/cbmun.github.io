Discover CBMUN

Honing, Refining and Redefining Diplomacy

We provide the opportunity for individuals to get their perspectives through to a large audience. CBMUN’s previous incarnation CBMUN ’18 delivered by allowing the delegates to Transcend Transform and Take down the world issues. Our exquisite maintenance of decorum means each delegate not matter how timid will get an equal opportunity to speak and will improve their public speaking skills. Here we will galvanize them into harnessing their true potential.

Meliorating the Art of international relations

An extraordinary array of exacting topics Committees setup with just the nations to incite intense discourse An experience simulating what being a true diplomat feels like, representing billions of lives. An aim to provide a better understanding of the world at large, so that we can take a step towards universal emancipation.

Celebrating the lighter side of life

A phenomenal array of socials. Delving in to the thought-provoking art of poetry through Bazam-e-Azb. Dance to the tune of soulful and youthful music with artists such as Abdullah Qureshi. After 4 days of intense debate you may feast like a diplomat at the Formal Dinner.
