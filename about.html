About us

A few words about us

On behalf of Institute of Business Management (IoBM), the Literary and Public Speaking Society (LPSS) is pleased to announce Pakistan’s one of the most academically sound conferences, CBMUN’19, scheduled to take place from February 22, 2019 to February 24, 2019 at IoBM Main Campus.

Held as an acting, three-day simulation of the United Nations, CBMUN’19 aims to provide the participants a valuable learning experience where they get a chance to bag freedom and channel their voices regarding global concerns and devise purposeful solution to them, by offering them to be a delegate of an assigned committee; representing a specific country and an agenda.

"Revolutionizing Discourse and Reinstating Diplomacy" as it states, our slogan stands for equity, legitimacy and success through creativity and hard-work.

The Literary and Public Speaking Society (LPSS) of IoBM is a student body whose prime agenda is to strive to engage students in various forms of oratory, which helps to bolster their delivery, two-way communication, confidence and reasoning ability. With a motto of ‘embracing diversity’, LPSS seeks to foster, promote, and hone the skills and interests of writers, orators, diplomats, and literary enthusiasts among the student body. We are a society which strives to organize events which cater to both the ‘literary’ and ‘public speaking’ aspects of the interests of students. While debating is our forte, we give equal importance to literature, poetry, and prose. Our team comprises of individuals which are well-read, well-versed, and well-informed about the intricate details and technicalities of public speaking.

What is an MUN?

Put simply, MUN stands for Model United Nation. It is a simulation of a series of sessions in United Nations where students represent various countries as their respective delegates and debate upon a specific problem affecting the international community. A prominent aspect of an MUN is that the focus is not only on the debates alone but also on how official debates are conducted, what rules and procedures are to be followed and what formalities are required to be fulfilled in order to proceed with the committee. An MUN provides the opportunity to be familiar with the given international topic and to harness your skills, as it demands meticulous research, the art of diplomacy and a high level of public speaking skills. The ultimate goal of an MUN is to pass a draft resolution, approved and voted by the majority, where possible solutions regarding the given issue are laid down with a proper set of instructions and framework.

What is an MUN?

It is essential to know that an MUN is not only an academic learning experience but also a recreational one. After the exhaustive debating sessions, participants eagerly await the socials. These usually include concerts, festivals, Beach trips, formal dinners and other various events. Socials play an important role in attracting the audience, however, the sole purpose of the socials is to promote the social interaction within the participants.

CBMUN '19

Our emphasis is not only on providing the best platform for the art of public speaking but also on transforming the youth of our country into potential leaders. While a standard Model United Nations merely focuses on the topics in United Nations, our aim is to provide quality research which is meticulously collected by our own experienced debaters and to properly guide our participants in coming up with viable and practical solutions for the various crises in the world. Indeed this very aim has been highly appreciated by leading debaters and professionals around the country.

This event's agenda is to not just put up a simulation of an MUN but to foster a motivational environment where the delegates do not only effectively engage in dissent, debate, and discussion but also simultaneously depict their assertiveness, confidence and self-expression to the respected committee directors, fellow peers and to themselves!
